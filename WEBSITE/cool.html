More about myself

Despite having a Romanian background, I actually grew up in Spain for most of my life. After finishing primary school, I moved to England and have settled here ever since, which means I’m quite the multicultural guy! This extensive background made me into a laid-back, open-minded fellow.


I won’t lie though, I’ve been into scary stuff since I was a kid, music included. I love deep, intense feelings, whether it’s extreme horror or the heavy blast of metal. I’m all about those strong vibes. And, of course, my favourite colour is red, if you didn’t notice LOL.

How Lunarium came to life

It all started at the end of 2021, a really tough time in my life. In the span of just one month, I lost two family members, which sent me into a serious depression. I needed a way to cope with all the mental pain, so I decided to channel it into something aggressive, something to release all those emotions. That’s when I started creating a horror project (check out the Artwork page for more details), and eventually, a few years later, it grew into what became Lunarium.


So.. what now?

I know the novel is still a long way from being finished. Between university, work, and everything else, it’s been tough to find the time. Developing this site also took a lot longer than I expected! But now that it’s done, I’m finally able to put my full focus into the novel. I want it to be something that really resonates, packed with emotions that hit deep. Once it’s ready and finished, I’ll make a big announcement and release it for everyone to enjoy! You’ll be the first to know when it’s up for purchase!
