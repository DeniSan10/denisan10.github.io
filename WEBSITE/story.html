Synopsis of LUNARIUM

Welcome to the synopsis page. Here you'll discover insights about the story, its roots, and the journey of The Curse of Lunarium across its expansive yet insane timeline.

The Revival of the Thousand Year Curse

Be prepared to uncover the twisted truth about those who unleashed the undead nightmare, who they were, why they did it, and the catastrophic consequences that turned the world into a graveyard crawling with the living dead.


The Origins

The story begins thousands of years ago, during the early days when the pyramids were just starting to take shape. This was long after the Gods had created what they considered the perfect reflection of themselves — humans — to populate the Earth and expand their divine vision.
Birth of Lunaris

Humans began to populate the Earth, spreading their influence and establishing homes, shelters, and territories. With their rise, however, came a heavy price, greed and conflict. Wars erupted, and bloodshed became rampant, spreading chaos across the land faster than anything else. The Gods eventually noticed the shift in human nature and decided to witness it firsthand.


This was the moment their greed was truly tested. The most powerful of all the Gods, Osiris, descended to Earth and crafted a stone with his own blood, beautiful yet unsettling in its eerie shine. This stone was far from ordinary; it held a liquid so obscure that its true nature defied human understanding. After its creation, Osiris commanded Khonsu, the God of the Moon, to spill his divine blood and infuse it to the moon itself, binding its power to the stone.


He called it...

The Thousand Year Eclipse


This event was inscribed onto a cursed papyrus under the name of Lunarium. Buried deep within the earth, it lay dormant, waiting for a worthy clan to uncover its secrets.

In the depths of Cairo, there lay a kingdom known as the Osirian Ascendancy. This clan was led by three powerful pharaohs who shared everything with everyone, earning them great respect. They were renowned for their wisdom and compassion, having established peace throughout all of Egypt. However, their harmony was disrupted when they met their eyes upon the prophecy, offered to them by a mysterious man...


Release of Lunarium

The pharaohs decided to keep the discovery a secret, concealing it from the other clans to ensure that no one else learned of the powerful prophecy hidden within the scripture.
The Final Betrayal

However, events took a darker turn when Ptahankh, one of the pharaohs, unexpectedly encountered the same mysterious man once more. This time, the figure made a sinister offer: a stone whose described powers eerily mirrored the ancient scriptures' prophecies, in exchange for his soul.


He hesitated, but accepted the deal.


Making his way back to his palace, he couldn't shake the feeling that something was following him at all times. He distanced himself from his peers and comrades, paranoid that they would discover his secret. It was his alone. No one had to find out.

Days had passed since he obtained the stone, and the Earth was consumed by darkness, drowning the skies in a haunting crimson hue. Ptahankh murdered his comrades in cold blood and consumed the serum he had crafted from the liquid.

His body began to melt, his form disintegrating into a grotesque display of corruption. Thunder clapped across the lands as his twisted transformation reached its peak. From within his very being, dark energies burst forth, unleashing hordes of undead that rose from the boiling sands, slaughtering all in their path. Weeks passed, and the curse seemed to vanish without a trace, as if it had never existed—leaving the world to wonder if the darkness had truly been lifted.


Return of the 'Gods'

Thousands of years later, a scientist uncovered a shard of a red shining stone. As he delved into its study, he discovered an entirely new element, one that defied all known science. This element held regenerative powers beyond imagination, excitement overtaking him during that same night, just asa he was about to sleep. Had he, at last, crossed the boundary between life and death?
Crimslate, Creators of Chaos

The following morning, the man mysteriously perished in his room. His body never to be found. A clan, known as Crimslate, was behind this.


They proclaim themselves as gods, wielding countless methods to peer into the afterlife. Who they are, and what they truly are, remains a mystery... but their power is undeniable.


For centuries, they amassed a collection of logs detailing methods to extend life and cheat death, believing this to be the key to their success. Their experiments, however, came at a horrific cost. Innocent civilians became their unwilling subjects, tortured relentlessly until their final breath.

They established a clandestine society hidden deep within the shadows of the dark web with the help of SphynxNet, where their influence spread like a virus.

With the prophecy's calendar foretelling the imminent arrival of the Thousand-Year Eclipse, their desperation reached new heights. How they came to know of this ancient prophecy remains an unsolved mystery... However, SphynxNet might hold the key to the truth..


Revival of Lunarium

SphynxNet, a formidable rival to Crimslate, claims to have witnessed the very start of existence. Their ancient records, marked by the symbol of their cursed eye, foretell the imminent return of the long awaited Eclipse. This celestial event, they warn, holds the power to unseal a long-banished curse, threatening to plunge the world into darkness once more.
The Eye of the Sphynx

A mysterious man, driven by unknown motives, chose to shake hands with SphynxNet and uncover their secrets on his own. Whether he was abducted, coerced, or simply deceived remains a mystery, but he followed them willingly, journeying all the way to Cairo on his own.


He settled himself there, waiting in uneasy silence. Then... he saw it, the Eclipse... bathing the earth in crimson light, drowning the world in blood once more.

That was his chance. He searched until... he noticed it, the eerie glow of the moonlight pointing in a specific direction. He followed it for hours. And then, there it stood... The Osirian Ascendancy, risen once more after thousands of years...


He forced his way inside the empire. The floor was littered with corpses—previous trespassers twisted in unnatural directions. Eerie whispers echoed from the walls, some warning him to turn back, others begging him to press forward. Was it all in his head?

At the peak of the ascension, he saw it: Ptahankh, clutching the Lunaris. The ancient corpse lay brittle and fragmented, its body decayed. Driven by some dark purpose foretold by SphynxNet, he grabbed his shattered remains, violently tearing the dried, cracked limbs apart. Somehow, he brought him back to life.. the line between life and death getting shattered for good.


The Beginning of the End

The curse unleashed itself upon the earth once more, the undead rising to their feet under the same cursed moon. But this time was different. The man responsible for this horror was identified by the government, though their efforts to contain him were futile. He singlehandedly eradicated every government force sent after him, including Ptahankh himself. This demon earned a codename: Cadaver-7, a national threat unlike any the world had ever seen.
The Only Ray of Hope

When all hope seemed lost, one man stood his ground, Dr Nihil, a brilliant mind renowned for his unparalleled knowledge of the undead. With relentless determination, he joined forces with the government to establish the Saint Mary's Task Force, a specialised organisation dedicated to combating the rising legions of Lunarium and uncovering the dark secrets surrounding Cadaver-7.


Many young men and women were drafted into the Task Force, undergoing specialised training to combat the relentless undead. While numerous undead had been successfully contained, the cost was steep... countless innocent lives had been lost to the curse. Weeks turned into months, and unlike the past, the Eclipse refused to release its grip on the Earth. What was Cadaver-7 doing to maintain the Eclipse, even after its anticipated end?

Things looked hopeless for the Task Force and the whole world. Cadaver-7 was on the verge of bringing the end of everything, his dark power growing stronger by the day. Would this be the time humanity's final breath would be taken, wiped out by the undead, or was there someone who could step up and restore peace to the Earth? Or perhaps, would Earth be left to wither, the undead left to starve after their rampage, consuming every living soul? The fate of the world now hung in the balance, and the clock ticked ever closer to midnight...
